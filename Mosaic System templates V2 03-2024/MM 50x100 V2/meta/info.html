Open source template for creating your own modules for the Mosaic system.

This project is licensed under the CERN OHL v2 W license.

Includes:

  • PCB with all the required fills and masks and the board outline
  • A selection of commonly used components to speed up the design process
  • Several graphics you can freely use

For more info and commercially available modules visit Mosaic synth.com

For any questions or to show me what you have built please feel free to email me at info@mosaicsynth.com
